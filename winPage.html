Game rules

  1. Choose a Funder. Everyone else is a Designer.
  2. The Funder receives a random Pitch Topic and Requirement and reveals it to the Designers.
  3. Designers pitch ideas to fulfill the Pitch.
  4. The Funder picks a favorite Pitch idea.
  5. If the Funder can't decide, they can use a random Wild Card to use as judgment criteria.
  6. The Funder role passes to the left.
